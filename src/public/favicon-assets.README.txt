CAM Initiative favicon assets

The site uses the transparent gold triskelion mark for browser favicons and the header identity mark.

Expected generated/public assets:
- favicon.svg
- favicon.ico
- favicon-48x48.png
- favicon-64x64.png
- android-chrome-512x512.png
- site.webmanifest

The transparent favicon assets are suitable for light and dark browser chrome. The Android 512px asset retains its supplied dark background for installed-app presentation.
